NukeZone Game Rules

A1. General

Two warnings of any kind results in a ban. Warnings do not have to be given prior to a ban. These rules may be changed at any time without prior notice. Additional conditions for your use of this service can be found in the End-User License Agreement that you accepted upon registration and Gold User/Gold Clan contract if applicable.

A2. Multiple Accounts

Only one account per individual is allowed. Account sharing counts as multiple accounts and is therefore not allowed. Account trading is not allowed. Multiple accounts or reasonable grounds to suspect multiple accounts result in an immediate ban of all involved accounts. We suggest that only one account should be used per PC to ensure compliance with this rule.

A3. Spam and Harassment

Spamming is simply the sending of irrelevant messages or messages that has no discussion possibilities. Spamming of any kind results in an immediate ban. Harassment and racist messages are in no way tolerated and results in an immediate ban. Harassment includes posts that attack others in a harmful way. Racism includes posts that attack others based on their race, religion or nationality. All spam and harassment messages that are sent to your inbox should be reported using the "Report this message" link, which is displayed at the bottom of the page while viewing a private message in your Inbox.

A4. Clans

The clan leader is fully responsible for all information found on the clan properties page including the content of the clan homepage. The Clan Name, Clan Tag, Clan Info, Clan Homepage content and Clan Logo must not contain any unsuitable material. Unsuitable material includes unlawful, harassing, abusive, threatening, harmful, pornographic, objectionable material of any kind or nature. Such material will be defined at the absolute discretion of the NukeZone crew. All clans with such material will be immediately deleted and the clan leader will be warned or banned without prior notice.

A5. Mass Invite

Mass invite provinces to your clan is strictly forbidden and results in an immediate ban and clan deletion.

A6. Alliances and Help

A clan alliance simply means any type of cooperation between players that are not in the same clan, or that are without a clan, in a permanent or a temporary form. The only communications that are permitted between clans are asking for a mutual war, asking for peace and arranging the start and/or finish details of a war. Any other forms of communication that discuss cooperation, what your own clan/province intend to do or asking the intent of another clan/province is strictly forbidden. Any attempt to create an alliance, whether successful or not, will result in an immediate ban from the game and clan deletion without prior notice. It can also include the additional banning of the Clan Leader and possibly the Co-Leader and one or more Trustees depending on the situation. The action taken in response to an alliance or report is at the absolute discretion of the NZ Crew and cannot be appealed. If you want to help another player, join the same clan. Joining a clan for the sole purpose of receiving a clan bonus is also considered cooperation and will be treated in the same way.

A7. Boosting

Sending aid to a specific player for the only reason to boost up that player counts as cheating and results in a ban from the game for all involved players.

A8. Automated Scripts and Bots

Use of automated scripts or other software that accesses the NukeZone game servers for any reason outside of normal gameplay is not allowed.

A9. Similar province names

Province names that are specifically designed to mislead, confuse, or make the ability to determine one province from another very difficult are strictly prohibited. Province names may have words in common as long as the intent is not to mislead, confuse, or make it difficult to determine one province from another. Violators will be warned and any further violations will result in a game ban.

A10. Bugs and Loopholes

All bugs, errors and loopholes should be reported in the Technical Support and Suggestions forum immediately when found. The act of exploiting bugs, errors or loopholes, which gives a province or clan an unfair advantage in gameplay, is strictly forbidden. Loopholes as defined by the NZ Crew on a case by case basis.

A11. Clan Information

All clan information fields must be in English. Target advertising a clan or province is not allowed if the intention is to only make another clan or province an attackable target. However, you may post your wins and losses in wars that have been fought. Swearing, harassing, or flaming is not allowed.

A12. Abuse towards the NZ Crew

Messages which are abusive towards or attack the NZ Crew, or any of its members, in any way are not tolerated. This includes messages posted in the forums, on the NZ Portal, through the private messaging system (including replying to forum ban messages or messages sent from Customer Support) and any other means of communication. Abusive messages are anything that contain content which is insulting, offensive, objectionable, or attack the actions the NZ Crew. Such material will be defined at the absolute discretion of the NZ Crew. The communication of such material results in an immediate game ban.

A13. Proxies

The use of proxy servers, except for those assigned by ISPs for normal use is strictly forbidden. This rule also applies to, but is not limited to, anonymous website proxies and any other means to hide your identity.

A14. Intent to Cheat

Attempts to gain advantage over other players through any means outside of normal gameplay, whether successful or not, are strictly forbidden. The intent to cheat is defined at the discretion of the NZ Crew on a case by case basis.
