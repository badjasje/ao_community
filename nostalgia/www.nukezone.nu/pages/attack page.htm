  Attack - Step 1 of 3 Guffy (#1)
 

To attack an enemy, enter the target province id or select your target from the enemy list (only available for Gold Users) and select the attack method, then press next to continue. Launch Missile costs 4 turns and uses 75% missile power. Regular Attack, Ground Attack and Air and Sea Attack costs 3 turns. Send Spy costs 1 turn. Send Thief costs 2 turns and 10% morale. Send Sniper costs 2 turns and 5% morale.

The Main Target and Attack Type options are only available when using Regular Attack, Ground Attack or Air and Sea Attack. The Main Target is also available for missiles and satellites.

You can attack provinces with a networth between $997 439 and $1 954 981. Morale of your army is at 20%. Your satellite power is at 100% and your missile power is at 100%.
