Buy Units - Sell Units - Orders

The market prices are higher than the normal price because it does not cost any turns to buy from the market, only money. You can however push the prices down by researching Market Discount.

Units bought from the market arrive 12 hours and 0 minutes later (6 hours and 0 minutes later in accelerated shipping mode), you can reduce the shipping time by researching Market Shipping Time or by using accelerated ordering mode which halves the shipping time but also increases the price by 1.5x.

You have a 30% discount on all units.
